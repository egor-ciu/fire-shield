Our mission

At Flame Shield, we're leaders in fire safety innovation. Our advanced fire alarm detection devices not only detect fires early but also pinpoint their exact location on a map through our cutting-edge software.

Our mission is simple: to save lives and protect property. We're dedicated to providing swift and precise fire detection and localization solutions.

What we do?

Advanced Detection Devices

We design and manufacture state-of-the-art fire alarm detection devices equipped with temperature and CO sensors.

Data Transmission to Control Center

The data collected by our devices is seamlessly transmitted to our central Control Center using secure and reliable communication protocols.

Precise Localization on Map

Our Control Center processes the incoming information and uses sophisticated algorithms to pinpoint the exact location of the detected anomaly on a map.

Instant Alerting and Response

In the event of a fire or hazardous condition, our system triggers instant alerts to the designated response teams, whether it's first responders, facility managers, or security personnel.
